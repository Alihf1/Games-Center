How to Play

Welcome to "The Ultimate Maze"! Your goal is to navigate the player through the maze to reach the finish point.

Controls:

  • Arrow Keys: Move the player Up, Down, Left, or Right.
  • On-Screen Buttons: Use the on-screen buttons to move if you prefer clicking.

Gameplay:

  • Select a difficulty level from the menu to start the game.
  • Use the controls to navigate through the maze.
  • A timer counts down the time you have left to solve the maze.
  • Be careful! On higher difficulties, the maze may rotate, and hidden obstacles may appear.
  • If you get stuck, you can surrender and return to the main menu.
  • You can enable or disable the AI opponent using the button at the top left.

Good luck and have fun!
